Planetary intelligence layer

Current planetary context: immature technosphere

Earth is treated as an immature technosphere overall: humanity has planetary-scale effects, but not yet mature planetary self-regulation. Country profiles below show relative readiness within that global condition. The country marker is not a biosphere-stage marker.

How to read this

Earth is fixed here as an immature technosphere. The bubble row describes the current global planetary context, not a country-by-country pathway.

Countries are subsystems inside that context. A selected country is scored for readiness within the present immature global technosphere.

The readiness score is not a national-IQ ranking. It is a transparent proxy for human capability, collective coordination, and planetary self-regulation.

Five diagnostics

The diagnostics follow the planetary-intelligence properties: emergence, networked information flow, semantic feedback, boundaries and signals, and autopoietic self-maintenance.

  • Emergence: capability arising above individual actors.
  • Networks: institutional and informational connectivity.
  • Semantic feedback: environmental signals become meaningful for action.
  • Boundaries and signals: planetary limits are detected and acted upon.
  • Autopoiesis: the system supports its own long-term conditions of existence.
